Who We Are....

At Public Transportation System (PTS), we are dedicated to providing safe, sustainable, and accessible transit services to the residents of Surat.

  • Integrated Transit Solutions : We seamlessly connect BRT, city buses, regional buses, and regional rail to ensure accessible mobility across urban and suburban areas.
  • Advanced Technology : Our Intelligent Transit Management System (ITMS) and Command & Control Centre enable real-time monitoring, rapid incident resolution, and improved operational efficiency.
  • Smart Ticketing : With our Automatic Fare Collection System (AFCS) and Surat Money Card, we offer smart, hassle-free ticketing that includes QR-based tickets and tap & go features.
  • Sustainable Impact : Our efforts have led to a significant modal shift from private vehicles to public transit, improved schedule adherence, and a remarkable reduction in carbon emissions.

Our mission is to make Surat an accessible and competitive city through a reliable, efficient, and eco-friendly transportation network that caters to the diverse needs of our community.

Passengers can now use digital smart cards for hassle-free ticketing and cashless transactions. This system also provides special concessions for eligible passengers, making public transport more accessible and convenient to everyone.

Know More

For Physically Challenged Eligible passengers can access scheme benefits by fulfilling, the following documentation and eligibility criteria for special concessions under the Smart Card System. Once approved, the Smart Card grants cashless travel, discounted fares, and seamless renewal options for continued usage. The below documents essential for the application process:

  • Category Proof: Disability Certificate issued and apporved by the government authorities (40% or higher disability required).

Safety Tips

Stay Alert

Be vigilant during your bus journey. Keep an eye on your belongings and report anything unusual.

  • Keep your personal items secure.
  • Be mindful of suspicious behavior.
  • Alert the driver or authorities if needed.

Maintain Distance

Practice maintaining distancing when boarding and riding the bus.

  • Stand in marked waiting areas.
  • Avoid crowding near the doors.
  • Follow seating guidelines onboard.

Wear a Mask

Wear a mask throughout your journey to protect yourself and others.

  • Ensure proper coverage over your nose and mouth.
  • Carry a spare mask if needed.
  • Dispose of or sanitize your mask after use.
