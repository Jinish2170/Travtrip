1. Definitions

1.1 “Personal Data”

Information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

1.2 “Processing”

Any operation performed on Personal Data, such as collection, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to data collected through our website, mobile app, and any related online services. It does not cover data collection by third-party sites or services linked from our Platform.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Contact details: name, email, postal address, phone number
  • Account credentials: username, password
  • Payment data: credit/debit card details (processed by secure providers)

3.2 Usage & Technical Data

  • Device/browser: IP address, browser type, OS, screen resolution
  • Location: approximate geolocation (with permission)
  • Activity: pages visited, search queries, timestamps, error logs

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide services: process bookings, manage your account
  • Improve experience: personalize content, recommend routes
  • Communicate: send confirmations, updates, customer support
  • Marketing: deliver newsletters and promotions (opt-in only)
  • Analytics: measure usage, perform technical audits
  • Compliance: prevent fraud, respond to legal requests

5. How We Share Your Information

  • Service Providers: payment gateways, hosting, CRM
  • Business Partners: affiliates, marketing agencies
  • Legal Authorities: to comply with court orders or laws
  • Corporate Changes: in merger, acquisition, or sale

6. Data Retention

We keep your Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined here or as required by law. Account data may be retained up to 5 years after account closure for compliance and dispute resolution.

6.1 Data Minimization

We collect only the data we need to provide services and regularly review our practices to minimize unnecessary data collection.

6.2 Storage Locations

Your data is stored on secure servers located within your region and may be backed up internationally under appropriate safeguards.

7. How We Secure Your Data

We implement industry-standard measures: SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, regular audits. While we strive for security, no method is 100% fail-proof.

7.1 Incident Response

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities within 72 hours, in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, web beacons, and local storage to:

  • Remember preferences and session state
  • Analyze traffic and performance
  • Deliver relevant ads (with your consent)

8.1 Managing Cookies

You can manage cookie settings in your browser; disabling them may affect site functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently done so, please contact us to have it deleted.

9.1 Parental Consent

Parents or guardians may contact us to review or delete data collected from minors.

10. Third-Party Links & Services

Our Platform may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their policies separately.

10.1 Embedded Content

Embedded content (e.g., videos, maps) from third parties may collect data about you; their policies govern this collection.

11. Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics and other tools to understand usage patterns. You may opt out via browser plugins or privacy settings.

11.1 Opt-out Procedures

To opt out of analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or adjust your browser preferences.

12. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your Personal Data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of marketing communications anytime via unsubscribe link.
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on your permission.
  • Request data portability in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes. Continued use of our services signifies acceptance.
